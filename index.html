Understanding the Core Medicare Components

🏥

Part A & B

Original Medicare

Managed by the federal government. Part A covers inpatient hospital care, while Part B handles regular outpatient medical insurance and doctor visits.

⚡

Part C

Medicare Advantage

An all-in-one private alternative option. It bundles Parts A and B, usually includes Part D, and often provides added vision, hearing, and dental coverage.

💊

Part D

Prescription Drugs

Optional standalone plans run by private insurance companies approved by Medicare to help subsidize and lower your prescription medication expenses.
